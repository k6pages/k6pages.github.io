News:


Renovation Work to Begin

With listed building consent having now been approved. We can now begin refurbishing the phone boxes ready for our opening exhibition. We’re also planning the exhibition programming for this year and will be confirming the first two exhibitions imminently.

Be sure to check the website again soon for details on these. If you’d like to be kept up to date with our progress and exhibitions then like us on facebook or alternatively sign up to our newsletter.


Crowd Funding Campaign Launched

We’ve now launched our crowd funding campaign as one final push to make the gallery a reality. We’re hoping that with your support we can reach our target to refurbish the phone boxes and deliver an exciting programme of exhibitions and events.

We’re using the website Spacehive for the campaign which is designed with small civic projects like this in mind. You can get involved in our campaign on the Spacehive website here. If you’d like to share the campaign on facebook or twitter or even by email then please do, that would be fantastic.


New Website


Welcome to our new website. We’ve spent some time with one of the world’s best web developers trying to make it easy to use and quick to access news and information from K6.

We’ll be updating the site regularly with any news and of course all you need to know about current and upcoming exhibitions.

It should work in all browsers and on all the usual devices, although if you do experience any technical problems or have trouble viewing the site then please let us know.
